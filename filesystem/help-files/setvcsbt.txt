
SYNOPSIS
    setstandbytime mins
DESCRIPTION
    Change the turbo pump standby time at the beginning of autostart
    routines, before turbo pump is switched to full throttle. 
    Defines the single loop of standby (low RPM) mode of turbo time.
    The time is saved in the flash filesystem such that after
     a reset or power cycle, the value is restored.

EXAMPLES
    setstandbytime 15
